Programs

Standard Plan

125$/Month

The Standard Program is designed to provide you with a solid foundation for your fitness journey. It includes personalized workout plans tailored to your goals, body type, and preferences. Our expert coaches will guide you through effective and challenging workouts, helping you improve your strength, endurance, and overall fitness. Additionally, you'll receive general nutrition guidelines to support your fitness goals.

Complete Plan

175$/Month

The Complete Program offers a comprehensive fitness experience, covering both workouts and nutrition. In addition to personalized workout plans, you'll receive detailed nutrition guidance to optimize your results. Our expert coaches will assess your current nutrition habits and provide ongoing feedback to help you make sustainable dietary changes. With the Complete Program, you'll have all the tools you need to achieve your fitness goals and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Premium Plan

225$/Month

The Premium Program is our most exclusive offering, providing you with an enhanced level of support and customization. In addition to personalized workout plans and detailed nutrition guidance, you'll have access to additional benefits. These may include weekly check-ins with your coach, live video sessions for real-time guidance and feedback, and priority scheduling for appointments. The Premium Program is designed for those seeking a higher level of personalization and accountability to achieve exceptional results.
